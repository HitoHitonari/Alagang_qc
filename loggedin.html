ALAGANG BAYAN:
 QUEZON CITY MUNICIPAL HEALTH INFORMATION SYSTEM

Welcome to Alagang Bayan, a centralized health information platform for the citizens of Quezon City. Access your medical history, track your vaccination status, and stay updated with the city's health programs.

ABOUT US

Introduction

Alagang Bayan Quezon City Health Information System is a digital initiative by the Quezon City Government aimed at transforming how health services are delivered and managed across the city.

Key Objectives

Designed with local residents and healthcare workers in mind, the system provides a centralized, secure, and user-friendly platform for managing patient records, scheduling appointments, and tracking vaccinations.

Commitment to Privacy & Security

At Alagang Bayan Quezon City Health Information System, we prioritize the privacy and security of your health data. We comply with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 and implement strict safeguards to ensure your information is protected, confidential, and accessed only by authorized personnel.

Your trust matters to us—and we're committed to keeping your health information safe at all times.

OUR VISION

To be at the forefront of health information management in the Municipality of Quezon City by providing efficient, secure, and easily accessible health data for every resident. Alagang Bayan envisions a future where health records and services are managed efficiently, empowering healthcare professionals and enhancing the delivery of public health initiatives for the well-being of our community.

OUR MISSION

To provide a centralized and dependable platform for managing health information in the Municipality of Quezon City. Alagang Bayan aims to support local healthcare services by offering a system that enables efficient patient registration, appointment scheduling, and vaccination tracking. Our mission is to enhance the delivery of healthcare through accessible, accurate, and organized health data that empowers both medical professionals and the community.

WHO WE ARE

We are DIT 2-1 Team 5, Alagang Bayan from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines, committed to enhancing health information management in the Municipality of Quezon City. Through Alagang Bayan, we strive to provide a reliable and user-friendly system for handling patient records, scheduling appointments, and tracking vaccinations, ensuring accessible and efficient healthcare support for every resident.
